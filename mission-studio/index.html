What We Build

🌐

Accessible Nonprofit Websites

Fast, beautiful, donation-ready websites built on modern architecture. We ensure every site meets strict WCAG accessibility standards, features multilingual toggles, and implements specific "quick exit" safety designs for survivors browsing from compromised devices.

Web Apps & Case Management

We design custom intake apps and resource mapping tools. By focusing on privacy-by-design principles, we separate Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from functional case IDs, ensuring social workers have the tools they need without generating massive data liabilities.

Data & Impact Dashboards

Using Power BI and secure automated pipelines, we convert your fragmented grant metrics and outreach logs into real-time visual dashboards. Tell your organization's impact story clearly and effectively to major donors and institutional partners.

Secure Internal Tools

From private volunteer training portals to highly restricted document clearinghouses, we construct digital spaces that run on absolute least-privilege access, ensuring collaborative work remains completely confidential.

Anonymized Impact Stories

The Shelter Website

The Challenge: A mid-sized rural women's shelter relied on a hacked WordPress site. Attackers had injected malicious ads, compromising the safety of victims looking for immediate physical extraction.

The Solution: We rebuilt a completely static, highly secure site unreliant on SQL databases. We integrated a persistent "Rapid Exit" button clearing browser history upon clicking, and localized the content into three languages natively.

The Impact: Zero downtime or security breaches over 24 months. Over 150 immediate safety queries processed securely.

The Legal Referral Portal

The Challenge: A legal advocacy coalition was using unencrypted Google Forms to ingest highly sensitive survivor histories, exposing them to massive liability and operational chaos.

The Solution: We developed a custom web app using an encrypted Azure backend. We abstracted the PII logic, ensuring front-line intake workers could assign case numbers without ever transmitting actual names across the wire.

The Impact: Removed 30 hours a week in administrative manual tracking, allowing the firm to take on 40% more pro-bono trafficking cases safely.

Our Technical Philosophy

We do not experiment with our clients' safety. The anti-trafficking sector requires enterprise-grade rigor, focused tightly through a trauma-informed lens.

  • 🎯 Modern, Clean Web Stacks: We eschew bloat. We use semantic HTML/CSS, Vanilla JS, and lightweight frameworks only when dynamic states require them. This ensures sites load instantly on older cellular networks.
  • 🛡️ Security & Privacy by Design: Following "Zero Trust" architecture principles. We implement advanced routing protocols via Azure and Node.js to guarantee least-privilege data access internally.
  • ♿ Accessibility (WCAG): A website that a visually impaired person cannot navigate is a failure of empathy. Strong contrast, screen-reader markup, and focus-states are mandatory in our builds.

About the Founder

My name is Shane Dennis. For over 15 years, I served intimately within military intelligence alongside units like the 1st and 5th Special Forces Groups. That environment forged my absolute commitment to accountability, precise data protection, and protecting the vulnerable at all costs.

Upon transitioning to the private sector as a full-stack cloud developer and AI engineer (specializing in Azure, Python, and Power BI), I recognized a massive disparity: massive corporations commanded millions of dollars in defensive cyber budgets, while grassroots organizations fighting human trafficking were left relying on insecure spreadsheets to manage the lives of trafficking victims.

I founded SafeHaven Digital Studio to bridge that gap. Every line of code my team writes is built on the unwavering belief that survivor dignity and physical safety come first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies for free services?

We strictly prioritize registered nonprofits, recognized advocacy coalitions, and verified shelters whose primary organizational charter is directed toward combating human trafficking or providing direct, long-term care to survivors.

How do you protect sensitive data?

We abide by "Privacy by Design." We abstract identifying variables, enforce multi-factor authentication everywhere, implement 256-bit encryption on transit and at rest, and advise organizations to never store unneeded demographic markers online.

How long does a typical project take?

A standard informational shelter website requires 4 to 6 weeks. Intricate web applications, highly secure case management portals, or complex Power BI data integrations range from 3 to 6 months depending on scope.

What do you need from our team to get started?

Minimal technical lift. We need a clear explanation of your operational bottlenecks, access to your brand assets (if any), and one designated point-of-contact who understands your organization’s privacy protocols.

How do you handle ongoing support and updates?

Depending on the SLA agreed upon, we offer reduced-rate retainers covering security audits, content updates, server hosting management, and emergency response pipelines.
